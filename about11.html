About Us

Gamcheon Culture Village, Busan, South Korea
Hello! Welcome to a very casual hikers, memories share space. As someone who is a major homebody, I am not an avid hiker but I do tend to enjoy my fair share of hikes. I have been hiking casually for the past 6 years throughout California. Over the years, I've been to quite a few places and I wanted to share my memories(especially pictures) with the rest of world. It's more enjoyable when it's shared afterall. I hope it inspires people to go visit it themselves.
